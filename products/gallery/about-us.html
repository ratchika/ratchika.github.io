About GREENKRAFTZ.ORG



GREENKRAFTZ.ORG is an organization dedicated to educate and promote sustainable living.
GREENKRAFTZ.ORG has dedicated years of research and effort and have worked on many projects to reduce carbon footprint and create Eco-Friendly Community.

Our Focus


  • Develope Eco-Friendly Alternatives to Plastic.
  • Natural Alternatives for daily used Chemical based Products.
  • Educate the importance of Eco-Friendly Life Style and Sustainable Living.
  • Preserve Ancient and Traditional seed varieties.
  • Preserve and Educate our Traditional Sustainable Practices.
