What is a Bamboo Toothbrush

A Bamboo Toothbrush is completely made of bamboo, Except for the bristles which are mostly made from vegetable oil and other natural polymers. Learn how it makes a world of difference when you start using a bamboo toothbrush HERE.

How to Dispose a Bamboo Toothbrush

Even though the bristles are Natural polymers, They may take more time to decompose that the brush body. So it is important to break the head off the Toothbrush and dispose them separately.
